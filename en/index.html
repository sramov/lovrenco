Welcome!

Kali is the biggest place on the island Ugljan which is part of the Zadar archipelago. It is the most famous fishing place on the Croatian coast. It’s situated on a hill and is dominant concerning fishing boats that are placed in two fishing harbors.

Narrow streets, old houses made of stone give a special mediterranean tone.

In the center you can find the baroque church of St. Lovre whose bell-tower is overlooking the whole place. St. Lovre is the saint-protector of Kali in whose honor every summer fishing nights are celebrated. Our apartments are situated near the beach in the beautiful bay called Mala Lamljana.

We have four one-room apartments to offer; they have radios, Sat/TVs, telephones, heating and a bathroom with a shower. The balconies are overlooking the beautiful nature and sea.

For those of you who are sport fans there is a football field and a basketball court near the apartments.

By contacting the local tourist office excursions to National parks Plitvice, Kornati, Krka or excursions on a a fishing boat can be arranged. For those of you who are interested in hiking we offer you National park Paklenica.

Our hospitality, beautiful nature, see-through clean sea and pleasant climate will make sure your visit stays unforgettable. We are looking forward to your stay in Kali and would be delighted and pleased to host it for you.
